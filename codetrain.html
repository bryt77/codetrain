Wondering What Codetrain Is About?

CODETRAIN is an initiative brought to you by UITS

This is a 2 year program. By the end, you will go from novice to a mid-level software developer. The first year has a focus on training in software development. While the second year narrows down on career services that are aimed at help- ing you launch your career in the best way. It consists of interview prep, technical interview workshops, an innovation challenge, and internships.

At Codetrain, we teach everything from scratch starting from the fundamentals. Our teachers are very friendly and they will assist you to understand everything that you learn.


Codetrain is a three-phase program - the 12 month training program, Magic program and our Fellowship program. Our 12 month training program is an intensive bootcamp during which we provide world-class teaching and mentoring to transform anybody (with even no prior programming experience) into a professional software developer and start a successful career. Students get one-on-one teaching, career advice, job interview training, individual and team projects experience in mobile and web technologies. After the 12 month training, hardworking students get the opportunity to be selected for next program called Magic.
